About Us

Tree Recruiting was founded by engineers and entrepreneurs from Stanford who aim to bring in depth technical understanding and diligence to technical recruiting. We serve to connect disruptive startups with gifted engineers. We put in-person interactions at the core of our methodology.

Why Us?

Stanford & Engineers

We are computer science engineers from Stanford. Our team is composed of talented engineers who can understand the requirements of the position and evaluate the technical skills of candidates. Others will ask you a list of coding languages to conduct a database search we will sit with you to understand your work.

Face-to-Face Interaction

We believe that face-to-face interaction and interpersonal relationships are at the core of successful recruiting teams. It all begins with understanding the culture and values that are important to your startup. Our work is based on meeting with candidates to understand their life goals, ambitions, and values beyond the code.

Proven Process

We follow a proven process that connects the right talent with the right startup. This begins with an initial meeting at your office to understand the culture of your organization. After due thorough research and technical due diligence, we introduce a small number of candidates through our pipeline who are willing to join your team.

Our Process

We use a proven recruitment methodology that aims to place candidates not just on technical capabilities but also cultural and personality fit. We leverage face-to-face interactions and personal connections in each step of our process.

Client Meeting & Position Brief

When you decide that you want to work with us we begin with setting up an initial phone call to have some understanding about your startup and the position you are looking to fill. If we believe that your startup and the position fit our criteria we will setup a meeting with you at your office. We ideally prefer to talk to the founder and direct report of the position to understand the position and company culture. Based on our meeting we will prepare a position brief and confirm with you.

Candidate Selection & Interview

Once we finalize the position brief with you we start our candidate selection process. It begins with our network of associates creating a long list of candidates. Then we conduct interviews with all those candidates and shortlist 3 candidates based on technical ability, personality and interest.

Client Meets Candidates

We present you a short-list of 3 candidates with their interview reports attached. Then we set interviews for you with the candidates you will be interested from the shortlist.

Reference Check, Closing, Follow-up

Once you want to move forward with one of our candidates we assist you in the reference check process. After references are confirmed we can further assist you on closing of the candidate. We believe that recruiting done right doesn’t end yet. We conduct follow-up calls in 1, 3 and 6 month periods both with you and our candidate.
